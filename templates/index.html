You've Walked Past Me So Many Times

Our debut 'with artist' artwork - Aotearoa's largest public installation - reclaims 100 abandoned traffic cones from Auckland's streets, transforming urban neglect into monumental art at Te Komititanga Square. Each cone embodies city mismanagement and creative rebirth. Scan QR codes to uncover origins, track journeys, and add to their story. Live for 24 hours, this intervention proves art belongs to everyone. Patronage is building the home for art.
